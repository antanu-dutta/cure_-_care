About Us

Get to know more about Cure & Care Consultants and our journey in Health Tourism.

Who We Are

Cure & Care Consultants are experts in "Health Tourism," providing consultancy to foreign patients looking for treatment options in India. We have been in this field for over half a decade and have assisted more than 500 patients from 15+ countries, including the USA, UK, Spain, and others. Our services aim to make quality healthcare in India affordable and accessible for all.

With India's growing prominence in the medical and tourism sectors, we aim to make the country the hub of Medical Tourism by offering top-notch services tailored to patient needs.

Our Expertise

Skilled Surgeons

We work with the best surgeons in fields such as Cosmetic Surgery, Obesity Surgery, Spine Surgery, Urology Surgery, Dental Treatments, and Cancer Management.

World-Class Facilities

Our partner hospitals and centers specialize in Ophthalmology, Cardiology, Dermatology, Orthopedic Surgery, Ayurveda, and more.

Affordable Healthcare

We ensure that medical care remains affordable, offering significant cost savings compared to other countries without compromising quality.

Patient-Centric Approach

We follow the ethos of "Atithi Devo Bhava" (Guest is God), ensuring every patient receives warmth and respect.
